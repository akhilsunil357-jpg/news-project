  1. Lionel Messi Historic Visit to India: GOAT Tour 2025

    Lionel Messi, the Argentine football legend, concluded his highly anticipated GOAT Tour of India 2025, visiting multiple cities including Kolkata, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and New Delhi.
